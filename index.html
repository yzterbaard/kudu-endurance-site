Built for busy professionals: lawyers, engineers, founders, and anyone who wants to ride longer, stronger, and leaner – without wrecking their health.

Training for durability, not just today’s power file.

Most cyclists are over-fat, under-recovered, and spending too much time in the wrong intensity zones. Kudu Endurance takes a longer view: we build fatigue resistance, robust metabolic health, and an engine that still works in hour five.

Durability over fireworks

Extend how long you can hold useful power, not just your 5-minute best. More resilient long rides, fewer “I exploded at hour three” stories.

Metabolic health first

Smart fuelling and a strong aerobic base, not permanent carb anarchy. Use carbohydrates strategically instead of living on sugar.

Stay unbroken, stay consistent

Engineer training loads around your real life so you can stay uninjured, hit your key sessions, and keep stacking weeks and months.

Who Kudu Endurance is for.

Serious riders with serious lives. You don’t have infinite hours, but you do have ambition – and you’re willing to train intelligently.

Time-crunched professionals

Lawyers, engineers, founders and finance people juggling long hours who still want to show up strong at big events and local races.

  • Gran fondos & sportives (L’Étape, Dragon Ride, etc.)
  • Efficient use of 6–10 hours/week
  • Structure around work, travel, and family

Gravel & endurance racers

Riders targeting UCI Gravel World Series events, long gravel weekends, and tough road races who need depth, not just top-end.

  • Fatigue resistance in hours 3–6+
  • Course-specific prep and pacing
  • Fuelling that supports performance and health

Technical MTB riders

Mountain bikers comfortable on technical terrain who want the fitness to match their skills for long days in big terrain.

  • Fitness for BikePark days & big climbs
  • Balancing intensity with recovery
  • Support for mixed road / gravel / MTB seasons
